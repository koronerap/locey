Locey for Google Sheets™

The ultimate AI-powered localization tool built directly into your spreadsheet. Translate your game assets seamlessly with context awareness.

Key Features

Batch Translation

Translate hundreds of rows instantly using state-of-the-art AI models like OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Anthropic. Retain the exact formatting of your original texts.

Context Aware

Provide developer comments (context) in a separate column to help the AI understand puns, character genders, or specific game lore during translation.

Smart Glossary

Enforce strict terminology. Locey reads your Glossary sheet to ensure proper nouns, weapon names, and special mechanics are never mistranslated.

JSON Export

Export your localized sheet directly into a nested JSON dictionary format, ready to be imported into Unity, Unreal Engine, or any C# backend.

Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)

Locey values your privacy. Your API keys (OpenAI, Gemini, Anthropic) are stored locally and securely in your own Google Workspace properties. We do not store your keys or your spreadsheet data.
