Privacy Policy for Locey
Effective Date: February 28, 2026

1. Introduction
Ahmet Erdoğan Akkulak ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our") operates the Locey Google Sheets™ Add-on (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the Service.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect
Locey is designed with strict privacy principles. We do not own any servers, databases, or backend infrastructure to store your data.

Spreadsheet Data: We do not read, store, collect, or transmit your Google Sheets data to any of our own servers. Your data is processed directly within your Google account and sent only to the third-party AI provider you explicitly choose (e.g., OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic, or DeepL) for the sole purpose of translation.

API Keys: Your personal API keys are never sent to the Developer. They are stored securely and locally within your own Google account using Google Apps Script's PropertiesService.getUserProperties(). Only your Google account has access to these keys.

Analytics & Tracking: We do not use any tracking pixels, cookies, or analytics software (such as Google Analytics) to monitor your usage of the Service.

3. Third-Party Services
To function, Locey transmits the text you select for translation to the third-party AI engine you configure (OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic, etc.). This data transmission is governed by the respective Privacy Policies of those third-party providers. We are not responsible for how these external AI providers handle your data.

4. Data Security
All data processing happens within the secure environment of Google Workspace. We rely entirely on Google's security infrastructure to protect your configurations and API keys.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
